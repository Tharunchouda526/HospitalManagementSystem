Hospital Rules and Regulations

The management of Keerthi Hospital wishes you a comfortable stay in the hospital and a speedy recovery. Kindly follow the hospital rules and help us in providing efficient services.

TOBACCO

All Tobacco products are strictly prohibited (Smoking, Chewing pan/pan masala) in the Hospital premises.

FOOD AND BEVERAGES

No Outside of Medical, Nursing, Housekeeping, and Maintenance staff are available round the clock. In case of any difficulty, contact your nursing station or the Public Relation Officer of Your Floor.

VISITING HOURS

Visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Please be considerate of patients' rest and privacy.

ADMISSION ADVANCE

Only one attendant is allowed with all categories of patients for the reason. Extra attendant is allowed in case of sick/critical patients at the discretion of NS/MS. Mattress with bedding will be provided for the attendant from 8:00 PM to 6:00 AM only, on payment of charges as per prevalent rates.

DISCHARGE

Check-out time is 12:00 Noon. Patients discharged between 12 Noon to 6 PM will be charged. Please clear your bills before 12 Noon and 6 PM.

AIR CONDITIONING

Please ensure that all windows/doors are closed for effective air conditioning.

VIOLence

Violence against medical, paramedical, other hospital staff, and damage to hospital property are punishable offenses.
